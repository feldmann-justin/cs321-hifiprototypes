Camera: Keeps logs of everything that happens while behind the wheel.
Diagnostics: This will allow you to identify any errors your car may be having for easier resolution
Weather: Plan a trip before you go. Check the weather forecast at home and at your desitination
HVAC and Seats: Hot or cold outside? Seat a little stiff? Fix it here!
Radio and CD: Want to listen to the newest hit single, or a classic? This is the radio and CD controls
Driver Mode: Eager to get the most out of your drive? You know best. Modify the preformance here.

Please practice hands free driving while using this, and any application. In order to make this app more driver friendly, voice and gesture controls have been programmed into it and are fully customizable. The home screen can be customized to provide quick access to widgets used daily.
